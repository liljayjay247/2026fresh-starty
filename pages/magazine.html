BACK IN BLACK

The 2018 Triumph Bonneville Bobber Black made a strong impression with its classic styling, blacked-out finish, and powerful performance.

Designed for riders who appreciate minimalist custom-bike aesthetics,

The Triumph Bonneville Bobber Black combines classic styling with modern engineering. Built around a minimalist silhouette, it delivers a raw riding experience while maintaining premium craftsmanship.

Blacked-out components, larger front forks, dual front brakes, and muscular proportions give the Bobber Black a commanding presence. The result is a motorcycle that feels equally at home parked at a café or carving through back roads.
